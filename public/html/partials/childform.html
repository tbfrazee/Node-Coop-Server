Doctor's Contact Information ? 
Doctor Information

Please provide the contact information for your child's primary care physician.

A babysitter may need to contact the child's doctor or may be requested to provide this information by medical personnel.

This is not the person a sitter will call in case of emergency. In case of emergency, your sitter will call 911.

Medical Conditions ? 
Medical Conditions

List any and all medical conditions (other than allergies, which will be listed below) that your child has, no matter how minor.

Click the button to add a new condition. Blank entries will be ignored.

For each condition, provide a descriptive, commonly-known name and instructions to handle any symptoms that may occur. Include medications to be used. You'll add more information about these medications in the next section.

This, along with allergy information, will be the first place your babysitter looks if your child presents with symptoms. Please be concise, but include all relevant information to handle a medical situation.
